About us:

Here you can read about us:

Push the comfort zone to get a healthier life. Hiking is a great way to get in shape and it can be healing to your mental health. RainyDays is here to make it easier for you to do it. It was founded in 2007 by Elisabeth, and is now in the world lead of outdoor clothes. You can`t blame it on the weather for not going outside with our jackets. We have something for every weather and trip. Our jackets are carefully made for every adventure. We been on a mission to make comfy and practical jackets. And along the way we been trying out every new jacket on our own hikes. We all love to explore new places in the world, and we would love to have you with us! When the business started we wanted to do more then just make jackets. So we started a community for everyone how love hikes and wanted to get healthier. And now we have people all over the world, who gets to gather and go on hikes. It`s truly amazing how this hole thing is coming together, so join us now! Find your favorite jacket in our collections and explore the world.
